DIETS

Gluten Free

A diet that avoids wheat, barley, rye, and other gluten-containing grains

Vegetarian

A diet without any meat or meat by-products

Vegan

A diet without any meat, eggs, dairy, or honey

Lacto-Vegetarian

A vegetarian diet where none of the ingredients can be or contain egg

Ovo-Vegetarian

A vegetarian diet where none of the ingredients can be or contain dairy

Ketogenic

The keto diet is based more on the ratio of fat, protein, and carbs in the diet
